Alvaro Pereira

Assistant Professor of Law · Georgia State University

I am an Assistant Professor of Law at Georgia State University, where I teach courses on corporate law and governance. My research focuses on how legal changes shape startup formation, venture capital, and the long-term sustainability of market institutions. Methodologically, I combine comparative analysis with empirical tools.

Areas of inquiry
Research

Corporate Law, Startups & Venture Capital

How corporate law frameworks shape the formation, financing, and governance of startups and venture capital relationships across jurisdictions.

Law & Sustainable Corporate Practices

Exploring the intersection of legal structures and long-term sustainability in corporate governance and market institutions.

Capital Markets

Analyzing the legal and institutional factors that drive capital allocation, market development, and investor protection.
